(on-table b1)
(in-hand b2)
(on b3 b1)

(unstack b3 b1)

Answer:

No, since (in-hand b2), b2 is in hand.

Yes, since no block is on top of b3.
3. Is (on b3 b1)?
Yes, (on b3 b1) is in the current state.

No, hand is not empty.
