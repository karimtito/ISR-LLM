Question:
Block current state: 
(on-table b1)
(on-table b2)
(on b3 b2)
Examined action:
(unstack b2 b3)

Answer:
Preconditions of (unstack b2 b3):
1. Is hand empty?
Yes, no block is in hand.
2. Is b2 clear?
No, since (on b3 b2), b3 is on top of b2.
3. Is (on b2 b3)?
No, (on b2 b3) is not in the current state.
Can we apply the action?
No, b2 is not clear to move.
