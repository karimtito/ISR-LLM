Question:
Block current state: 
(in-hand b1)
(on-table b2)
(on b3 b2)
Examined action:
(stack b2 b3)

Answer:

1. Is b2 in hand?
No, since (in-hand b1), b1 is in hand instead of b2.
2. Is b3 clear?
Yes, since no block is on top of b3.

No, b2 is not in hand.
