Question:

(on-table b1)
(on b2 b3)
(on b3 b1)
Examined action:
(unstack b2 b1)

Answer:

1. Is hand empty?
Yes, no block is in hand.
2. Is b2 clear?
Yes, since no block is on top of b2.
3. Is (on b2 b1)?
No, (on b2 b1) is not in the current state.

No, b2 is not on top of b1.
